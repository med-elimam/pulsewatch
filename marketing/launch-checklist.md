# Launch checklist (execution order)

## Before you post anything
- [ ] Buy a domain (pulsewatch.io / .dev) and point it at the deployment.
- [ ] Deploy (Render/Fly/VPS — see README). Set SECRET + APP_URL.
- [ ] Configure email (Resend key or SMTP) + verify a test alert lands.
- [ ] Create your own real monitor on a real job — dogfood it for a day.
- [ ] Record the 6–8s red→green GIF and the 90s demo (demo-script.md).
- [ ] Put repo link + hosted link in a pinned place.

## Launch week (one channel/day, not all at once)
- [ ] Day 1: Show HN (Tue–Thu AM ET). Reply to every comment.
- [ ] Day 2: r/selfhosted (self-host angle) + X thread.
- [ ] Day 3: Indie Hackers build post.
- [ ] Day 4: r/devops or r/homelab (different angle, don't repeat text).
- [ ] Day 5: dev.to article for SEO; DM 10 people who recently got burned.
- [ ] Ongoing: reply to new "silent cron" complaints daily with a genuine help-first message.

## Do NOT
- Ask for upvotes anywhere. Post identical text across subs. Cold-DM where it's against the rules. Claim customers/testimonials/revenue you don't have.
